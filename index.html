    Özellikler

    The custom-made traditional Mallorcan fabric used in this collection was created by ARTESANIA TEXTIL BUJOSA, a team of local master craftsmen that have perfected their techniques since they started the family business in 1949, to produce one of the island’s most characteristic crafts.

    “Telas de Lenguas” made in Santa María del Camí in Mallorca, is a handmade weave made with a mixture of cotton and linen which typically uses blurred geometric shapes in bold colours on a white background. Commonly used for interior decoration and upholstery, the hard-wearing fabric has a traditional yet contemporary characteristic.

    <b>Wool</b><br>This wool originates from the UK and New Zealand which have the highest standards of animal welfare and freedom.

    <b>Lifetime Guarantee</b><br>We believe that a truly sustainable product is one that is made of quality materials so that it lasts a lifetime and can be repaired. This shoe comes with a lifetime guarantee.

    This product is circular. That means that you can return it to us at the end of its life to be ground down to restart life as a new outsole or shoe. By creating circular products we are working towards our aim to eliminate waste to landfill.

    <b>Recycled Textile</b><br>In order to eliminate virgin plastic from our uppers by 2022, we are using recycled nylons and polyesters in our collection. These are more environmentally friendly textiles made from waste such as plastic (PET) bottles and industrial waste.

    <b>TENCEL™</b><br>TENCEL™ branded lyocell fibers are derived from sustainable wood sources and produced in an environmentally responsible closed loop production process. By incorporating both natural and botanic fibers we are eliminating the use of virgin plastic alternatives.

    <b>Better Cotton</b><br>Our aim is for all our cotton to be sustainably sourced by 2022. Currently, we’re using cotton from a mix of better sources including recycled and organic cotton and cotton from more responsible farming sources.

    <b>Leather Working Group Certified</b><br>Uses leather from tanneries rated by the Leather Working Group (LWG). The LWG assesses and promotes sustainable environmental practices across the leather industry. We are committed to using only LWG approved leather by 2022.

    <b>A Little Better, Never Perfect</b><br>Our goal to continually improve our product and reduce our impact. To be considered A little Better, a shoe must have at least 50% of its composition using better materials whilst maintaining the same standards of quality, durability and comfort that we always expect. Our aim is that all shoes fit this criteria by 2025.

    Leather Working Group (LWG) tarafından tabaklanan deriler kullanır. LWG, deri endüstrisi genelinde sürdürülebilir çevresel uygulamaları değerlendirir ve teşvik eder.
